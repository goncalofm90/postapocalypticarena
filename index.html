This world is....rotten....

Choose a wanderer.

Jacob

A former renowned military officer. Now an independent bounty hunter, Jacob will look to no means to get his bounty and his payday. The way he sees it,it's survival of the fittest in this new age.

Tyler

The best example of the unforgiving nature of the New Land. The burned man, known only as Tyler , a name taken from a book he read. He has no memory of anything from his past except the trauma of being burned by bandits after trying to save an old woman from being mugged and mercilessly killed.

Alexander

The remaining light of hope in the dark age we face. An extremely brilliant scientist who used to work for the government in the before times. Everyday is a struggle to forward his research in search of a cure for the New Land. The objective is to make it more fertile and also erode the radiation created by the Big Bang.

Helen

One of the best shots in the New Land. A former national guard officer whose only remnants of her old family life is her loyal companion, Scrub.

Iris

A former bartender turned sheriff, Iris has used her people reading,manipulation and adaptability skills as well as her unforgiving nature to rise in the ranks of Metal Town's security force. She doesn't care about means to an end and she will do whatever it takes for her law to be abided.
